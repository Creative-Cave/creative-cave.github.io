About the Creative Cave

The Creative Cave came to be at the beginning of December 2021, branching off another server which was due to close.

The server, then called Writer's Cave, was made partly to preserve the community of the server that preceded it, and partly to open a space where writers could come together to share their work.

Since then, Writer's Cave grew into the Creative Cave, broadening the scope of the community to include all kinds of creators at all kinds of skill levels and bringing a wide range of artistic mediums together.
